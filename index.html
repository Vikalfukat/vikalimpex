About Vikal Impex

Your Dedicated India Sourcing Partner

Vikal Impex is a professionally managed Indian export house based in Nagpur, Maharashtra — the geographic heart of India. We specialize in sourcing and exporting Agro Products, FMCG Goods, and Non-Medical Consumables to verified importers, distributors, and retail chains across five major global markets.

Our model is built on three pillars: rigorous product sourcing from FSSAI-approved, ISO-certified manufacturers and farms; destination-market compliance so your cargo clears customs without surprises; and a single, accountable point of contact throughout your entire order lifecycle.

How It Works

From Inquiry to Delivery in 6 Steps

A transparent, accountable process designed to give international buyers full visibility at every stage.

01
Send Inquiry
Share product name, quantity, destination port, and packaging requirements via the form, email, or WhatsApp.
02
Quote & Samples
Formal quotation within 48 hours with spec sheets, HS codes, and FOB/CIF pricing. Samples dispatched within 5–7 days on request.
03
Order Confirmation
Confirm PO with agreed terms. Proforma Invoice issued. Payment: TT Advance, LC at Sight, or 30%+70% BL.
04
Sourcing & QC
Product sourced from certified facility. Pre-shipment inspection conducted. All compliance documents prepared by our team.
05
Shipping & Documents
Container booked, cargo loaded. Full document set couriered: BL, COA, COO, APEDA, Invoice, Packing List, Phytosanitary.
06
Delivery & Reorder
Real-time tracking shared. Post-delivery support for customs queries. Dedicated account manager for seamless reordering.
