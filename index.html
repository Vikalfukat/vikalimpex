About Vikal Impex

Your Dedicated Sourcing Partner in India

Vikal Impex is a professionally managed Indian export house specializing in the global trade of Agro Products, FMCG goods, and Non-Medical Consumables. We serve importers, distributors, wholesalers, and retail chains across five major international markets — partners who demand consistent quality, reliable documentation, and responsive supplier relationships.

Our operational model is built on rigorous product sourcing from certified Indian manufacturers and farms, full regulatory compliance with destination-country import standards, and end-to-end logistics support that ensures your cargo arrives on schedule, correctly documented, and in pristine condition.

How It Works

From Inquiry to Delivery in 6 Steps

A transparent, reliable process designed to give international buyers full visibility at every stage of the trade.

01
Send Inquiry
Share product name, volume, destination port, and packaging requirements via email or WhatsApp.
02
Quote & Samples
Formal quotation within 48 hrs. Samples dispatched within 5–7 business days on request.
03
Order Confirmation
Confirm PO with agreed terms. Proforma Invoice issued. Payment terms: TT Advance, LC, or as negotiated.
04
Production & QC
Product sourced or manufactured. Pre-shipment inspection conducted. All documents prepared.
05
Shipping & Docs
Container booked, goods loaded. Full document package sent: BL, COA, COO, Invoice, Packing List.
06
Delivery & Support
Real-time tracking shared. Post-delivery support for any queries or follow-up orders.
